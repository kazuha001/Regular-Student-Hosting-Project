HE CARES MISSION
BACOLOD

MISSION

He Cares Mission is dedicated to being a beacon of hope for homeless, wounded, and vulnerable children in Bacolod City. We believe every child deserves a chance to recover, learn, and thrive. Through comprehensive care, access to education, and opportunities for personal growth, we empower them to overcome challenges, build self-sufficiency, and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

VISION

A future where all children in Negros Occidental, especially those who are abandoned and at-risk situations, are empowering with thriving members of their communities that equipped with the education, skills, and faith to reach their full potential and contribute positively to the world.

ABOUT THE FOUNDATION

WHO ARE WE

The He Cares Mission began in 2016 and was led by volunteers from the light of Jesus. This foundation serves children ages ranging from five to eighteen years and the He Cares Mission - Bacolod found at the kid's ministry of the Jesus Feast where street children near the venue of their gathering. The staff from Manila came in full force of Bacolod. Bro. Joe Dean met with the Bacolod Team and shared about the missions, visions, and programs of He Cares. All program servants were present to render support because this was a great opportunity to come onsite with about more than hundreds of kids who were handled by the volunteers.

BOARD
OF DIRECTORS

Joe Dean Sola

Joe Dean Sola emerges as a compassionate leader, deeply motivated by his faith. This passion likely fueled by his decision, alongside his wife Ardis, to establish He Cares Mission in 1996.

Ardis Sola

Ardis Sola is a co-founder and admin head of He Cares Mission. Both of her husband did full-time lay missionaries who came from light of Jesus, even started He Cares Foundation Street Children Caring Center in 1997.

Judith Enriquez

Judith Enriquez is an admin assistant who handles administrative duties like creating documents, reports, and presentations, and may even assist with bookkeeping or light accounting depending on the company's population.

STAFF/
VOLUNTEERS

Daryl Tamon

Daryl Tamon is a full-time back-to-God missionary for He Cares Mission after a whole year of immersion. His decision is not easy as he faces with worries, mainly regarding finances. He has been with the foundation for 6 years and counting.

Madonna Galang

Madonna Galang is a full-time back-to-Work missionary that seeks to provide families of street children with livelihood opportunities and sustainable income. The parents who are part of the Balik- Hanapbuhay Program were trained in such respective fields.

Sheila Rose Dino

Sheila Rose Dino is a full-time back-to-Health missionary that aims to offer regular quality feeding for the street children as well as health care through daily medical and dental check-ups and other means of support as needed.
